Step 5: Last Minute Details

 

 

 

 

 

IMPORTANT!

Leaving enough time for last-minute details will help you feel more relaxed and able to enjoy your party!

  • Create "set-up" plan (if necessary).
    • Know where you plan to put extra tables and chairs.
    • Know where decorations go and double check you have enough.
    • Know where you plan to serve the food and beverages. Where are the serving dishes and/or chafing dishes being placed?
  • Schedule time for any rental pick-ups or deliveries.
  • Schedule time for food prep
  • Schedule time for caterer delivery/pick-up. Don't forget the cake!
  • Schedule time for decorating. Do you have to pick-up balloons?
  • Schedule time on Party Day (or day before) to head to store for last-minute whatever's.
  • Schedule time to freshen-up immediately before party.
