Step 1: Pre-Planning

 
Choose the Date and Time

To consider:

  • Is party related to a specific event tied to the calendar? Birthday, Sporting Event (i.e. Super Bowl, or Memorial Day
  • How much planning time is required?
  • Are certain people required to be there? Check their schedules.
What is the budget?
  • Is the event large enough to warrant budget consideration?
  • A little effort here can save a lot of disappointment!
  • For most events, a detailed budget is not necessary.
Danger! Skipping this step will most likely have negative consequences!
