Step 3: Invitations

 

 

 

 

 

Electronic or Mailed?
  • Paper invitations and stamps cost money.
  • Paper invitations are less likely to go unnoticed.
  • You will have leftovers.
  • Must hand track R.S.V.P.'s
  • Electronic invitations are convenient.
  • Electronic invitations still require "design" time.
  • Electronic invitations might go unnoticed (i.e. junk folder or recipient notifications are not set)
  • R.S.V.P.'s are electronic too!
  • Ability to easily send reminder.
To Consider:
  • Must create guest list
  • Must gather addresses (email or home).
  • Create or pick out invitation (electronic or paper)
  • Send at least 2 weeks prior to event.
Expectations Alert! Don't count on R.S.V.P.'s! Many won't commit.
