Step 4: Supplies

 

 

 

 

Larger Items

Here are some larger items you might own. These are also available for rent at your local rental center or party store.

  • Folding Tables
  • Round Tables
  • Folding Chairs
  • Chairs
  • Coolers - do you need extra?
  • Event Tents
  • Shade Tents
  • Chafing Dishes
  • Beverage Dispenser (for the kids?)
  • Portable Bar

Don't forget to consider borrowing items from friends / neighbors.
